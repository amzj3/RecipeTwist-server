Cherry-Topped Chicken
Ingredients
4 boneless skinless chicken breast halves (4 ounces each)
1 tablespoon canola oil
1/2 cup cherry preserves
1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
Directions
Flatten chicken to 1/4-in. thickness. In a large skillet, cook chicken in oil over medium heat for 4-5 minutes on each side or until no longer pink.
Meanwhile, in a small microwave-safe bowl, combine preserves and allspice. Heat in the microwave until warmed. Serve with chicken.
